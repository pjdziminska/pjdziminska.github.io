I create print as art + art as fashion.
Combined with extended use of mixed media, limitless imagination and a dash of VHS nostalgia you get Front Lawn Utopia

Born and bred in post-industrial Łódź, Poland.
I have moved to England to study Fashion Textiles and Accessories at DeMontfort University. There I focused on print / pattern, re-discovering textiles as an art form. Currently based in Liverpool, I take inspiration from the sharpness of technology and softness of nature
